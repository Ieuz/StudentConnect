Help & FAQ

What is StudentConnect?

StudentConnect is a plarform to help students in Glasgow connect with eachother!

How do I sign up?

You can sign up here. You will be prompted to answer a few simple questions, and we will match you with other students!

I forgot my password! What should I do?

You can request a password reset on our login page.

How do I connect my social media to my account?

In your profile page, click the 'Connect Social Media' button. You will be prompted to enter your information there!
